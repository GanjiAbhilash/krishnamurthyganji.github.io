The Story

A Life of Perseverance

From a humble family in Adoni with no legacy of education or wealth, to one of the highest ranks at India's premier coal mining company — this is a story of grit, determination, and unwavering spirit.

27 July, 1965

Born in Adoni

Born to Shri Ganji Achanna and Smt. Nagamma in Adoni, Kurnool District, Andhra Pradesh. A small town boy with big dreams, from a family with no history of formal education or material wealth.

Schooling

The Foundation Years

Studied at Ramakrishna Matam School and later at TG High School, Adoni. Even in those early years, his dedication to academics stood out — the first in his family to pursue education with such commitment.

College

BSc & Engineering

Completed BSc at Arts & Science College, Adoni, then went on to pursue Engineering at the prestigious Vasavi Engineering College, Hyderabad. A monumental achievement for a first-generation learner.

1989

The Career Begins

After a brief stint at Alwin Freezers, Hyderabad (4 months), joined Singareni Collieries Company Limited on 9th August 1989 as a Junior Engineer Trainee. The beginning of a 38-year legacy.

26 May, 1991

Marriage

Married Smt. Chiluveri Maha Lakshmi — a partnership of love, strength, and shared values that has stood strong for over 35 years.

1989 – 2025

Rise Through the Ranks

Through sheer dedication and technical excellence, rose from Junior Engineer Trainee to Additional General Manager (E&M) — the highest possible grade — earning 7 promotions across 38 years.

31 July, 2026

A Glorious Retirement

Retires as Additional General Manager (E&M) at SCCL, leaving behind a legacy of excellence, integrity, and inspiration for generations to come.
